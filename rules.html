服务器条款(Server Rules)


概述

HwanTai Games(以下统称“我们”) 所有游玩者(以下统称“您”)需共同遵守以下条款。

在游玩服务器之前，请您首先阅读MINECRAFT 最终用户许可协议 ('EULA')，这是您与我们都应该遵守的，不可违背的第一条款。

第一原则

如“MINECRAFT 最终用户许可协议 (‘EULA’)”中所提到“我们的价值观”一样：
1.Minecraft 适合每个人
2.多元化是我们社区的动力
3.与他人一起玩游戏应该是安全和包容的
4.这里没有仇恨的容身之地
这个服务器适合所有人（被HwanTai Games列入黑榜的除外），在一起进行游戏是，您不应该对其他玩家（此指与您有仇恨的玩家）进行言语侮辱、游戏中刻意针对。

违规处罚

您需要知道，在服务器中，您并不是不受管制的，服务器内有管理人员与作弊处罚机器，您的言行举止需要注意。

1.作弊处罚
1.1.在游戏中，使用修改器对游戏进行速度修改、面容更改、操控他人、更换游戏模式的，将处1个月的有期徒刑。
1.2.在游戏中使用连点器或其它物理工具使您的鼠标速度加快的，将处15天的有期徒刑。
2.言行举止处罚
2.1.在游戏中辱骂他人及其亲属超过三次的，将处15天有期徒刑。

以上“有期徒刑”将会在您的HwanTai Games档案上留下，以后您进行HwanTai Games的游戏时，将会受到一些限制：充值金额比无案底人更加昂贵、“无法游玩聊天游戏、每小时聊天句数限制为1条、无法组队聊天、无法加入群组（仅限于触犯第2条款的玩家）”。

are the same as "our values" mentioned in the MINECRAFT End User License Agreement ('EULA'):

2. Diversity is what drives our community
3. Playing with others should be safe and inclusive
4. There is no place for hate
This server is suitable for everyone (except those blacklisted by HwanTai Games), and when you play together, you should not verbally insult other players (this refers to players with whom you have hatred) and deliberately target them in the game.

Violation penalties

You need to know that in the server, you are not unregulated, there are managers and cheating punishment machines in the server, and your words and actions need to be paid attention to.

1. Punishment for cheating 1.1.
In the game, anyone who uses a modifier to modify the speed of the game, change the face, manipulate others, or change the game mode will be sentenced to 1 month in prison.
1.2. Anyone who uses a linker or other physical tool to speed up your mouse during the game shall be punished by 15 days in prison.
2. Punishment for words and deeds
2.1. Anyone who insults others and their relatives more than three times in the game will be sentenced to 15 days in prison.

The above "imprisonment" will be left on your HwanTai Games profile, and when you play HwanTai Games games in the future, you will be subject to some restrictions: the deposit amount is more expensive than that of the undocumented person, "cannot play the chat game, the number of chat sentences per hour is limited to 1, the number of chat sentences per hour is limited, the group cannot be formed, and the group cannot be joined (only for players who violate clause 2)".
